=== Pirate Battle Game ===

Welcome to the Pirate Battle Game! Your goal is to defeat powerful enemies in different levels.

Use the left mouse click to shoot and press ESC to display the pause menu.

=== Levels ===

Level 1: Usopp vs. Lucci
   - Shoot Lucci 10 times to proceed to the next level.

Level 2: Luffy vs. Doflamingo (Coliseum)
   - Hit Doflamingo with Luffy's punches 10 times to proceed.

Final Level: Luffy (Gear Fourth) vs. Blackbeard
   - Click rapidly on Blackbeard before he defeats you.

Complete the game as quickly as possible to achieve victory!
